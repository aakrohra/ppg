Welcome! All the Personal Projects created by the 2020-2021 grade 10 students of GFSS can be found here.

About the Personal Project: The Personal Project is an opportunity for MYP students to explore an area of personal interest over an extended period of time. It provides them with the tools to consolidate their learning and develop skills that they will need in further education alongside life beyond academics. It also helps students become principled, lifelong learners.

Students picked one of six global contexts to use as guidance for their Personal Projects.

1. Identities and Relationships
2. Orientation in Space and Time
3. Personal and Cultural Expression
4. Scientific and Technical Innovation
5. Globalization and Sustainability
6. Fairness and Development

For more information on the MYP Personal Project, visit this page.
